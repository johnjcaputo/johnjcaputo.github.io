What we’ve been up to lately

Our recent adventures took us to explore the ruins of a papermill at Sope Creek and camping at Fort State Mountain Park.

We love the outdoors. BSA Cub Scout Pack 422 can be found around Georgia on hikes, camping trips, and service projects. At our home base of St. Catherine of Siena, in Kennesaw, we meet serveral times a month. We are always engaged in:

- having fun
- learning Scouting skills
- fellowship
- recognizing the achievement of our Scouts
- service to the community
